John and Tex's Dog Door - What the Door Does

1. Tex barks to be let out.
2. John hears Bruce barking.
3. John presses the button on the remote control.
4. The dog door opens.
5. Bruce goes outside.
6. The dog shuts automatically.
6. Bruce does his business.
7. Bruce barks to be let back inside.
8. John presses the button on the remote control.
9. The dog door opens (again).
7. Bruce goes back inside.
8. The door shuts automatically.
