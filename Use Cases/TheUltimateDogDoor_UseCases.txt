Todd and Gina's Dog Door - What the Door Does

Main Path:
1. The owner's dog barks to be let out.
2. The Bark Recognizer "hears" a bark.
3. If the bark came from the owner's dog, the Bark Recognizer sends an Open request to the dog door.
4. The dog door opens.
5. The owner's dog goes outside.
6. The owner's dog does his business.
	6.1 The door shuts automatically.
	6.2 The owner's dog barks to be let back inside.
	6.3 The Bark Recognizer "hears" a bark.
	6.4 If the bark came from the owner's dog, the Bark Recognizer sends an Open request to the dog door.
7. The dog door opens (again).
7. The owner's dog goes back inside.
8. The door shuts automatically.
	
	
Alternate Path:
1. The owner's dog barks to be let out. 
2. The owner hears their dog barking.
3. The owner presses the button on the remote control.
4. The dog door opens.
5. The owner's dog goes outside.
6. The owner's dog does his business.
	6.1 The door shuts automatically.
	6.2 The owner's dog barks to be let back inside.
	6.3 The owner hears their dog barking (again). 
	6.4 The owner presses the button on the remote control.
	6.5 The dog door opens (again).
7. The owner's dog goes back inside.
8. The door shuts automatically.
